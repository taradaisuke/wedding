Getting to the Hotel

From Dublin station by train

From Heuston station take the Westport service and change at Manuilla junction. Take the Westport service to Ballina. From Ballina you can take a taxi to Ennis crone (the taxi will take about 15 minutes)

From Dublin airport by bus

Go to zone 11, stop 2. Take the expressway 22 bus to Ballina. From there you can take a taxi to Enniscrone (approx travel time is about 4.5 hours)

From Knock airport

Take the number 64 towards Galway bus station. Change at Ballysadare (about 50 minutes). From there, take the 458 towards Ulsterbus depot Enniskillen. Get off at Enniscrone (about 40 minutes)
